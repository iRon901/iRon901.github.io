Testing Equipment

IN-HOUSE QC LAB

We invest in industry-standard testing equipment to ensure zero compromise on quality — from incoming raw materials to finished product.

🔧
Torque Tester
Used to test torsional strength of screws under real installation conditions. Ensures drive recess integrity and head-to-shank durability on every production run.
🧪
Salt Spray Tester
Salt spray tester for testing plating and extended coatings to ASTM B117 standards. Validates long-term corrosion resistance for all surface finishes.
💎
Hardness Tester
Rockwell hardness testing of screws and bolts to verify correct heat treatment and confirm material grade matches specification on every production lot.
📐
3D CAD Software
Used for geometric verification of customer drawings and samples. Ensures dimensional accuracy and tolerance compliance before production tooling is confirmed.
⚡
Hydrogen Embrittlement Tester
Detects delayed defects on hardened screws — critical for safety-related structural applications where unexpected fastener failure cannot be tolerated.
🔩
SDS Drilling Tester
Used to test the drill-point performance capability of Self-Drilling Screws on standard steel substrates. Confirms point geometry and penetration rate meets specification.
