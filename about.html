Our Story

MANUFACTURER &

Charng Yuh is a manufacturer & exporter of industrial fasteners in Taiwan (R.O.C.) since 1995. For more than 30 years, we have developed relationships of trust and respect with our customers from the global market.

Whether you are an importer, OEM, ODM, wholesale distributor, or industrial manufacturer, we understand the best way how to meet your needs — in service, innovation, and quality — to create a win-win approach in long-term relationships.

We act with integrity in all we do. You can expect nothing less when doing business with us. A strong relationship will be built not only with us, but between you and your own customers.
